Research

Here I have written some brief explanations of research, both original and not, that I have done.



Non-Ergodic Billiards

Under the supervision of Dmitry Turaev


This was a project wherein I looked at the way the non-ergodicity of a dynamical billiard could lead to unbounded energy gain.



Entanglement Entropy in Causal Set Theory

Under the supervision of Yasaman Yazdi


This was a project on causal set theory, a theory of discretised spacetime. I studied a covariant entanglement entropy definition, wherein the entanglement entropy is not defined relative to some Cauchy hypersurface.



Picard-Lefschetz Theory

Under the supervision of Job Feldbrugge


This is the project I am currently working on, studying the usage of Picard-Lefschetz theory to define real time path integrals, avoiding the use of a Wick rotation.



Seiberg-Witten Theory

Under the supervision of David Tong


This was the topic in which I wrote my part III essay, which was about the Seiberg-Witten solution to Seiberg-Witten theory. I covered preliminary and neccessary topics to understand the solution, such as solitonic solutions in gauge theory, and the basics of elliptic curves. I also briefly went on to cover how the Seiberg-Witten solution gives rise to confinement, which is the only known analytic means by which we have confinement within a quantum field theory.
