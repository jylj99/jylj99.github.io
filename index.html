Joshua Y. L. Jones

I am a phD student at the Dublin Institute for Advanced studies, under the supervision of Yasaman Yazdi. Before that, I completed Part III in DAMTP at the University of Cambridge, taking courses on theoretical physics. Before that, I was an undergrad at Imperial College London.

At Imperial, I carried out my final project under the supervision of Yasaman Yazdi. I have also been doing some work outside of my formal education with Job Feldbrugge, who is at the University of Edinburgh.
